About GymPlayer

The GymPlayer project explores how humans interact with reinforcement learning environments. Here, you can play short episodes in classic Gymnasium tasks: Controlling agents, exploring dynamics, and seeing how your own strategies perform.

Your rewards are tracked to compare human performance with machine learning agents, helping researchers understand how people learn and adapt in complex environments.
